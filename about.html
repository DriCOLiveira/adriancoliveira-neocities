About Me

Hi, my name is Adrian, and I'm an Electronics Technician with a passion for technology and innovation. Currently, I'm pursuing a degree in Systems Analysis and Development, where I'm exploring new ways to apply my skills and knowledge.

Throughout my studies, I've worked on some exciting projects, including designing and building my own power supply and using Arduino to generate an image and display it on a monitor for a math-based game, also running on the same Arduino board. Being interested in games and anime, I've sometimes explored game development and drawing skills. Although I'm still a beginner in these areas, it would be cool to learn more and improve my abilities.

As someone who's always been fascinated by technology, I'm excited to put my skills into practice in new projects. Whether it's developing innovative software solutions or contributing to the open-source community, I'm grateful for the opportunities I've had so far, and I look forward to the many more that lie ahead.
